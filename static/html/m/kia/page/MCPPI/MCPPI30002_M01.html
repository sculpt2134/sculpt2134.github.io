Guidance Notice
  • You can change repayment frequency and due date up to twice during the term of the loan.
  • The interest accrued due to due date change will be included in the interest on the billing statement for the next payment.
  • If you change repayment frequency and due date after the billing statement has been sent, you need to make a payment for the payment number on the old due date.
  • Due date change is available from 08:30 to 23:00.
