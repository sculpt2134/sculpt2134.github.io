Financial Hardship Assistance
HardShip information
  • Online hardship applications are the fastest way to have your application assessed.
  • We appreciate that this is a difficult time and will do our best to assess your application as quickly as possible.
  • Depending on your circumstances, you may be required to provide supporting documentation such as a medical certificate, proof of income and expense, employment termination letter, etc;
  • Detailed and accurate information helps us assess your application faster;
  • We will contact you if we require any further information;
  • You can expect a response within 21 days.
