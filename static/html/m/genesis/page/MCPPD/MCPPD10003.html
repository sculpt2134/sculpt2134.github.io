Period
36M / 48M / 60M
Repayment method
  • At maturity, the residual value of the vehicle is estimated and applied as a deposit, and the remaining amount is repaid in installments over the contract period of 36, 48, or 60 months.
  • At the expiration of the contract, the deposit is returned at once according to the vehicle coverage rate, or the deposit is repaid by returning the vehicle.
Recommended Customer
  • Customers with a relatively short vehicle replacement cycle of 3 to 4 years
  • Maintain low monthly payments for as long as you use the vehicle
GFV
A repayment method in which the used car price is subtracted in advance and only the remainder is divided.
